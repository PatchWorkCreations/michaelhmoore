About the Author

Michael H. Moore shares a lifetime of wisdom, resilience, and faith gained through his remarkable journey as a sailor, veteran, and writer.

Michael H. Moore

Michael Moore is a U.S. Navy veteran whose life has been profoundly shaped by his years of service and his deep love for the sea. As a sailor and war veteran, he knows firsthand the courage and faith required to navigate life’s storms—both literal and metaphorical.

His experiences form the heart of The Sailor’s Compass, a memoir where Michael shares the wisdom he has gained on his journey through service, resilience, and self-discovery.
